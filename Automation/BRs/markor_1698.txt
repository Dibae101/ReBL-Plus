Bug Report Title:
Crash and exit after rotating the screen twice during searching

Bug Report Issue:
App crash and exit after rotating the screen twice during searching.

### Description
The app crashes when rotating the screen twice (once counterclockwise, then once clockwise) during a search operation.

### Steps to reproduce
1. Open Markor
2. Click to search
3. Input a word in the search box
4. Rotate the phone once counterclockwise
5. Rotate the phone once clockwise
6. Click Enter or continue searching
7. The app crashes and quits

### Information
Android version: Android 8
Device: Google Pixel 4
App Version: 2.8.6
Source: Google Play

### Expected behavior
The app should remain stable and functional after screen rotations during search.

### Actual behavior
App crashes and exits after rotating the screen twice during searching.

Comments:
Issue was reported on May 11, 2022 and fixed by May 21, 2022.
Bug affects version 2.8.6.
