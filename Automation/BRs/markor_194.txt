Title: NullPointerException when rotating device during filesystem dialog

App: Markor
Version: v0.3.2
Package: net.gsantner.markor
Issue: https://github.com/gsantner/markor/issues/194

Description:
The app crashes with a NullPointerException when the device is rotated while the filesystem browser dialog is open. This occurs when inserting an image and browsing the filesystem, then rotating the device.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Markor app
2. Create or open a document
3. Click on "Insert image" option
4. Click "BROWSE FILESYSTEM" button
5. Enable auto-rotate on the device
6. Rotate the device (portrait to landscape or vice versa)

Expected Behavior:
The filesystem dialog should properly handle the configuration change and redraw without crashing.

Actual Behavior:
The app crashes with a NullPointerException when trying to access the FilesystemDialogData$Options.cancelButtonEnable field.
